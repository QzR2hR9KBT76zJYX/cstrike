Rats: Bomb/Defuse
Creator: Chris Spain
Original Release: July 1, 2000

Description:
This classic map is in a small kitchen, and
all players are the size of rats. Counter-
Terrorists spawn inside the freezer and
Terrorists spawn inside the pantry. 

Terrorists:
Plant the bomb behind the sink and prevent
the Counter-Terrorists from defusing it.

Counter-Terrorists:
Eliminate the Terrorists and prevent the bomb
from blowing up the kitchen!
